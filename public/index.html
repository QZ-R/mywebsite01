Biography

Qing-Zeng Zhu is a Postdoc of organic geochemistry at the Hinrichslab. His research interests include stable isotope probing (SIP), organic matter (OM) fate in marine sediments, and geomicrobiology. He is currently working on a project about secondary production and its role in reshaping the OM pool after the deposition from primary production in the ocean.
